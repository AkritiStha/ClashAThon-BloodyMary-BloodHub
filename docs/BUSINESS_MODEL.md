# Business Model Canvas (BMC)

The business model is outlined in the components BMC. This BMC describes how the BloodHub creates, delivers, and captures the values as a social impact technology platform for Nepal's healthcare system. The points down below are the BMC components and details for the BloodHub:

## 1. Key Partners

- Nepal Red Cross Society
- Medical Colleges/blood banks
- Telecom Providers (SMS – future)
- NGOs in healthcare space
- Government hospitals & MoHP

## 2. Key Activities

- Blood request creation & matching
- Instant email donor notification
- Dashboard monitoring for hospitals
- Scaling and maintaining platform
- Verification of the donor and onboarding

## 3. Value Proposition

- 85%+ request fulfillment rate
- Zero costing for the donors
- Real-time hospital dashboard
- Saves the life of more than 1500+ peoples /year (Nepal)
- <30 min response time

## 4. Customer Relationships

- Automated alerts on the email
- One-click donor responses
- Self-serve dashboard of the hospital
- Community trust building
- Future: push notifications + SMS

## 5. Customer Segments

- Blood banks and Hospitals
- Voluntary blood donors (18-65)
- Medical College
- Emergency departments

## 6. Key Resources

- Donor and hospital database
- Node.js API server
- Open-source development team
- Email notification system
- Cloud infrastructure

## 7. Channels

- Web application (Netlify)
- Email (Nodemailer/Gmail)
- Social media awareness
- Hospital outreach & partnerships
- Future: mobile app (iOS/Android)

## 8. Revenue Streams

- Free for donors (social mission)
- Subscription / SaaS fee for hospitals (future)
- Government or NGO grants
- CSR partnerships with pharma
- Premium analytics dashboard for hospitals (future)

## 9. Cost Structure

- Cloud hosting (Railway)
- Email service (Gmail SMTP / free tier)
- Marketing & hospital partnerships
- Future: SMS gateway costs
- Development & maintenance effort
